Potato Salad

potato salad

Description

The recipes for potato salad

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: 2 pounds, preferably Yukon Gold or Russet
  • Mayonnaise: 1 cup
  • Mustard: 1 tablespoon (yellow or Dijon)
  • Hard-Boiled Eggs: 4, chopped
  • Celery: 2-3 stalks, chopped
  • Red Onion: 1/2, finely chopped
  • Pickles: 1/2 cup, chopped (optional)
  • Salt: To taste Black
  • Pepper: To taste
  • Paprika:For garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the Potatoes: Peel and cube the potatoes. Boil in salted water until tender (about 15-20 minutes). Drain and let cool.
  2. Prepare the Dressing: In a large bowl, mix the mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and black pepper. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Add the cooled potatoes, chopped eggs, celery, red onion, and pickles to the bowl. Gently mix until everything is well coated with the dressing.
  4. Add Optional Ingredients: If using, mix in dill, green onions, bacon, apple cider vinegar, sour cream, and parsley.
  5. Chill and Serve: Refrigerate the potato salad for at least an hour before serving to let the flavors meld. Garnish with paprika if desired.
